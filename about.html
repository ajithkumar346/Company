    Our Mission

    To achieve sustainable growth and customer delight through exceptionally committed professionals, striving to impart global standards, by adhering to our ways of work and core values.

    Embedded Systems:-To establish end to end research and service operation centre in India.

    Recruitment:- To establish ourselves as a preferred hiring partner for 80% of the client base.

    Training:-To obtain national and international accreditations for our training and as well as establish us as renowned trainers for technical and soft skills arena.

    Our Vision: To be a global leader, being one stop solution to our clients, through continuous and consistent efforts of our synergetic, dedicated and optimistic team, in order to raise leaders for the future

    Company Overview

    Standing true to our tag line we are working towards raising leaders for the future. We are currently serving more than 30 colleges and 40+ companies with 4 ongoing projects on research and development.And we are proud to be the first and the only Bangalore based Multinational Research and Development Company for embedded systems. The services we provide include embedded systems – research and development, SAP consultation, college and corporate trainings and recruitment consultation. We are currently in the process of entering into strategy alliances and exclusive partnerships with a variety of technology and industry leaders including the top colleges in the country. In most cases Optimism IT Solutions will be supplying the much needed talents to our alliances, thus reducing the operating costs and allowing them to be more cost sensitive. We work with the key executives at each of these companies to understand the company initiatives that will help each of us to lower the cost and enhance the ability to meet time-to-market objectives.

    Company History

    Since its inception in 2012, Optimism IT Solutions has climbed many a ladder to accomplish what it has become today. Starting as Real Talentz, with just recruitment and consulting, the firm soon diversified into software and web development in the same year, in the process being rechristened Blue Park IT Solutions. Empowering and enabling fresh as well as experienced talent get right to their dream jobs with great ease. In early 2017, secure and highly optimistic in the knowledge of embedded systems, the idea of sharing this knowledge led Blue Park IT Solutions into the exciting realm of training as well. And finally, out of this immense confidence and experience, rose the powerhouse today known as Optimism IT Solutions.
